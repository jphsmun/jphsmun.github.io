AIPPM

The All-India Political Party Meeting are conventions that allow India's various political organizations to reach an agreement on issues of national importance prior to a parliamentary session or the presentation of a bill. Because the parliament lacks legislative authority, the main goal of the forum was to allow for unrestricted political discussion, debate, and deliberation, which may not be possible in parliament owing to time constraints. This body today helps to provide a deeper understanding of national concerns. Indo-China relations, while marked by historical ties and economic cooperation, also feature strategic competition, especially in the Indian Ocean. The Maldives, strategically located, has become a focal point due to Chinese involvement, prompting India to strengthen its ties there. As both countries vie for influence, their approaches in the Maldives will significantly impact regional dynamics and the balance of power in the Indian Ocean.
